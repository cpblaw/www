  • Acting for aviation hull all risks and war risks reinsurer in a series of claims relating to civil aircraft that were not returned from Russia to their lessors following commencement of the Ukraine war.
  • Advised reinsurer on aggregation of losses under aviation hull war risks treaties arising from damage sustained to aircraft during airport attacks by militias in North Africa.
  • Advised overseas brokers regarding several direct / facultative reinsurance claims under war risks and political violence property insurances and reinsurances, generally involving Middle East based insureds/reinsureds and losses.
  • Acting in a marine hull war risks insurance claim, involving an attack using mines on an oil tanker in the Gulf.
  • Acting in reinsurance claims for the insurer of commercial property damaged by political violence in Syria.
  • Facultative terrorism reinsurance coverage dispute arising from damage to industrial buildings in Syria.
  • Advising on political violence reinsurance coverage of insured losses arising from an arson attack.
  • Represented insurers in relation to reinsurance allocation and aggregation issues arising from the 9/11 terrorist attacks in USA.
  • Acting for insurers under a political violence policy and its reinsurance, in relation to damage to an industrial facility in East Africa.
  • Acting in a kidnap & ransom claim arising from events in Central America.
  • Advising a healthcare insurer on its policy cover relating to medical services in war zones.
